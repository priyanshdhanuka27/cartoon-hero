Olaf's Characteristics

  1. Sprung from Elsa's magical powers, Olaf is by far the friendliest snowman to walk the mountains above Arendelle
  2. His innocence, outgoing personality and uncanny ability to disassemble himself at good and not-so-good times lead to some awkward, albeit laughable moments
  3. He is innocent, outgoing and loves all things summer
  4. Olaf may be a bit naive, but his sincerity and good-natured temperament make him a true friend to Anna and Elsa.
  5. He LOVES warm hugs!
